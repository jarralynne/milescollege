Welcome to Prior Learning Assessment Planning

What is Prior Learning Assessment (PLA)?

PLA allows you to potentially earn college credit for knowledge gained through work, military service, certifications, and life experiences.

‚ú® New: Request Multiple Courses at Once!

You can now request credit for up to 6 courses on the same form. This saves you time if you have learning experiences that cover multiple courses.

Important Policy Information

  • Maximum Credits: Up to 30 semester hours through alternative credit
  • Residency Requirement: Must complete minimum 30 hours at Miles College
  • Official Transcripts Required: All prior college work must have official transcripts sent to Miles College
